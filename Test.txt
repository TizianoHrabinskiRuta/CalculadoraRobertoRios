VAR I SUBNETID 192.168.0.0
VAR I BROADCASTID 192.168.10.255
VAR I Subnetmask 255.255.240.0

VAR I RESULT1 0.0.0.0
VAR I RESULT2 0.0.0.0
VAR I RESULT_SUBNETID 0.0.0.0

PRT I RESULT2
PRT I RESULT_SUBNETID
